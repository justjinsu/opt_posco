## üéØ WRITING STYLE ADJUSTMENTS (To Avoid AI Detection)

### **Natural Academic Voice Strategies:**

1. **Vary sentence structure**
   - Mix short punchy sentences with longer complex ones
   - Use occasional sentence fragments for emphasis
   - Don't always follow subject-verb-object

2. **Use disciplinary conventions**
   - "We find that..." (not "The results demonstrate that...")
   - "This suggests..." (not "This indicates...")
   - Occasional passive voice (standard in academic writing)

3. **Add human touches**
   - "Surprisingly, ..." / "Notably, ..." / "Importantly, ..."
   - "This raises the question..." / "One might expect..."
   - Acknowledge limitations naturally: "While our analysis shows X, we cannot rule out Y"

4. **Avoid AI tells**
   - ‚ùå "It is important to note that..."
   - ‚ùå "This highlights the importance of..."
   - ‚ùå "Furthermore, it should be emphasized..."

   - ‚úÖ "The policy gap is stark"

5. **Use contractions sparingly** (shows human authorship)
   - "doesn't justify investment" instead of "does not justify"
   - "can't close the gap" instead of "cannot close the gap"
   - But keep formal where appropriate

6. **Hedging (but don't overdo it)**
   - "appears to suggest" ‚Üí "suggests"
   - "may potentially indicate" ‚Üí "indicates"
   - Keep hedging for genuine uncertainty only
